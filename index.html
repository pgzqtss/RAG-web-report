Features

Have a look at the amazing features of our application designed for medical researchers and doctors.

  • Upsert Chunks into Vector Database

    Medical research papers are split into smaller chunks, embedded into vector representations, and stored in a vector database to enable efficient semantic search.

  • Generate Systematic Review

    Given a research question or prompt, the system retrieves relevant context from the papers and uses an LLM to generate a structured systematic review with sections like background, methods, and conclusions.

  • Quality Check

    Using cosine similarity bar chart, TF-IDF heatmap, and BLEU score bar chart assess the relevance, key thematic alignment, and linguistic quality of the generated text in comparison to user input medical papers. Additionally, a word-cloud visualization highlights thematic areas for quick and intuitive validation.
